Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and plans

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and you'll only pay the difference for the rest of your billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card is required to start your trial. You can explore all features during this period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and wire transfers for enterprise plans.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer special pricing for registered non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Contact our sales team for more information.

Can I cancel at any time?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time. When you cancel, you'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer custom enterprise solutions?

Yes, our Enterprise plan is fully customizable. We can tailor the platform to your specific needs, including custom integrations, security requirements, and SLA guarantees.

Still have questions? Contact our sales team for personalized assistance.
